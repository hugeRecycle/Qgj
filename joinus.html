MAPLE GROUP
"Exploring the frontiers of human endevors in unknown spaces of arts, sciences and engineering is the goal of Westlake University. Together, we strive to push the frontlines through learning, researches, and practices."

Professor Qi Guojun's Machine Perception and Learning Laboratory (MAPLE, http://maple-lab.net/gqi/) focuses on deep learning-based generative artificial intelligence models and algorithms, multimodal computing, computer vision, and graphics. It is applied to the research and development of digital media, virtual people, virtual content and virtual reality.
